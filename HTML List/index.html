Introduction with list

        There are two types of list. They are:
        i) Ordered list
        ii) Unordered List

        
        Both Ordeered and Unordered List have an own style to represent it. Unlike, for Ordered list, it may
        i) Numeric (1,2,3,........)
        ii) Alphabetic 
            a) Capital (A,B,C,........)
            b) Small (a,b,c,..........)
        iii) Roman Number
            a) Capital (I,II,III,.....)
            b) Small (i,ii,iii,.......)

        And for Unordered list, it may
            i) Bullet
            ii) Circle
            iii) Disk
